Mobile App Development with Flutter

This course will teach you the complete process of developing mobile applications with Flutter, from the fundamentals to advanced topics

This programme will provide you a comprehensive foundation in building mobile applications using the Flutter framework. Flutter is a versatile and rapidly growing technology for creating cross-platform apps with a single codebase, and this course will equip you with the skills to develop high-quality mobile applications for both Android and iOS.
Prerequisite:
* There is no coding experience required for this course but participants must be computer literate and know how to navigate their way around it.
* A laptop with at least 4GB ram
* Passion for coding
Training Methodology:
Multiple training delivery methods are employed including Lectures, Demonstrations, Practical, Self Practice and workshop using real life case study with particular reference to Nigerian situations.
Duration:
Regular
* Four (4) months, twice a week.
Accelerated
*
